What is Temp Mail?

Temporary email (or disposable email) is a service that provides a short-lived email address. It is used to receive confirmation links or download files without exposing your real email to potential spam.

Ultimate Privacy & Security Guide

In today's digital world, your email is your digital identity. Exposing it to every website you visit is dangerous. Here is why online privacy matters:

Phishing Protection

TempMail shields you from phishing links. If an attacker gets your temp address, your real mailbox remains hidden and safe.

Spam Defense

90% of internet traffic is spam. By using disposable emails, you keep your primary inbox 100% clean and organized.

Data Privacy

Websites sell your email to advertisers. With TempMail, they only get data that will eventually disappear.

Privacy & Compliance

Effective Date: October 2025

1. Data Sovereignty

We do not store your data beyond the temporary session. Once you generate a new address or clear your cache, previous data is permanently purged.

2. Encryption

All traffic between your browser and our servers is encrypted using Industry Standard TLS/SSL.

3. Cookies

We only use essential functional cookies (LocalStorage) to maintain your session.

Privacy Glossary

Phishing:

A cyber attack that uses disguised email as a weapon to steal sensitive data.

TLS/SSL:

Encryption protocols that ensure data sent between a browser and a server is private.

Data Purge:

The permanent removal of data from a storage system so it cannot be recovered.

Digital Footprint:

The trail of data you leave behind while using the internet.
